Message From Principal

Welcome to our college

I am really pleased to welcome and extend my words of encouragement to all candidates who come forward seeking admission into various courses in Barama College. Established in 1971 and situated in an enviable and charming atmosphere, Barama College has traversed a long way of 52 years of its existence. The College has grown up into a glorious field of higher education during its long journey with the best academic atmosphere. In the process of its basic exercise, Barama College has accomplished a milestone in the field of higher education in the greater Baksa area of Assam. Today the college is constantly striving to offer quality education aiming at nurturing knowledge, skill and attitude in individuals to build them responsive to the changing socio-economic and cultural realities of the present society.
